## General TPA Writing Learnings

### What Works Well
- Specific examples over general statements
- Connecting to board priorities (PDSB specific)
- Balancing praise with acknowledgment of practice
- Referencing student outcomes/behaviors
- Including collaboration with colleagues/families

### What Needs Refinement
- [ ] Rotary/specialist teachers (current learning)
- [ ] Teachers with limited student contact time
- [ ] Technical/vocational language
- [ ] Evidence synthesis from bullet points

### Style Preferences Observed
- Formal but warm tone
- Mr./Mrs./Ms. + Last Name throughout
- Evidence-based statements
- Alignment with Ontario curriculum
- Equity and inclusion language
